Plane Embed Instructions

To embed in Plane:

  1. Go to Plane Settings → Views → Create View
  2. Name: "Workflow Runs" or "Workflow Editor"
  3. In custom HTML, add:
<iframe
  src="http://YOUR_COGNETIVY_HOST/embed/runs?projectId=${project.id}"
  width="100%"
  height="600"
  frameborder="0"
></iframe>
